AI Infrastructure for the Modern Web

As AI models grow more capable, the infrastructure layer becomes the competitive moat. Teams that build on flexible, scalable primitives will ship faster and iterate more confidently than those locked into monolithic solutions.

article hero image

The shift from batch inference to real-time streaming changed everything. Users expect sub-second responses, contextual awareness, and seamless handoffs between model generations without re-prompting.

Edge deployment closes the latency gap for global audiences. When inference runs close to the user, the interaction feels native—not like a network call. This is the baseline expectation for 2026.
